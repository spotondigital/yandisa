About Us

Company Background

Yandisa ICT Group is a service-driven digital technology company specialising in multi-cloud solutions, technology transformation, and strategic consulting. The mission of our technology consultants is to enable organisations to navigate and lead in the digital age through innovative, scalable, and outcome-focused solutions.

Our commitment extends beyond service delivery. We invest meaningfully in talent development and ecosystem growth. Through structured internship and graduate programmes, we support the entry of new talent into the technology sector.

In a world defined by relentless digital disruption, enterprises can no longer afford to stand still. At Yandisa ICT Group, we help our clients not only keep pace with change but also lead it. We design and deliver modern, resilient, and intelligent technology solutions that enable you to grow with confidence and clarity.

Whether you’re embracing multi-cloud, embedding AI into your operations, optimising cloud costs, or enhancing system reliability, we’re here to help you evolve, sustainably, and together.

At our core, Yandisa ICT Group is a cloud-native transformation partner, built to support organisations navigating complex digital journeys. With experience and expertise across financial services, investment management, gaming, and logistics, our team brings the strategic depth and technical expertise to deliver at scale.

We believe that the most sustainable transformations are the ones built on deep collaboration and practical execution. Our model is lean, partnership-driven, and built for speed, enabling us to move quickly, integrate deeply, and deliver measurable impact. Reach out to our cloud computing consultants today to learn more about how we can help your organisation meet its goals.

EVOLVE

We empower organisations to accelerate their digital journey through innovative ICT solutions, cloud technologies, and strategic expertise that deliver measurable business outcomes.

SUSTAINABLY

Our solutions are designed for long-term success—secure, scalable, and adaptable to evolving business needs. We also invest in people and partnerships to strengthen the future of the ICT industry.

TOGETHER

Collaboration is at the heart of everything we do. We work alongside our clients as trusted technology partners, delivering solutions that create lasting impact and shared success.

“Yandisa ICT Group is more than a service provider. We are a team of thinkers, builders, and enablers. We’ve helped South Africa’s top financial institutions transform their technology environments, and we’re expanding that capability globally.”
