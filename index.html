About Us

Company Background

Yandisa ICT Group is a service-driven digital technology company specialising in multi-cloud solutions, technology transformation, and strategic consulting. The mission of our technology consultants is to enable organisations to navigate and lead in the digital age through innovative, scalable, and outcome-focused solutions. We approach challenges with a forward-thinking mindset, leveraging deep expertise and a unique delivery model to provide tailored responses to complex business needs.

Through structured internship and graduate programmes, we support the entry of new talent into the technology sector.
